Stage Platform and Service Controller

The Stage is a special service that layers the platform as the services controller. A platform can be any device or software that presents interactive multimedia and gaming applications. Target applications include, Interactive Toys, AI, AR/VR, IoT, 2D/3D, Robotics and UI devices. Devices can be consoles, suchas Nintendo Wii/DS/Switch, Sony Playstation to PC/Mac/Linux to Android, iOS phines and tablets to Arduino. In addition, software platforms such the browser (Edge, Chrome, Safari, FireFox) and gaming development tools (Unit, Unreal) can have stage treatments. Typically the Stage will service the platform specifics such as timing and operation system events then run services in sequence to manage a frame cycle.


Services and the Service Agent

A service is a black box that the stage will prompt to process once a frame cycle. Content communicates indirectly to a sevice by service defined content objects, called Agents, aggregated by content Actors.
